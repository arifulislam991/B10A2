    Fitness

    Fitness refers to the state of being physically healthy and strong, achieved through regular exercise, proper nutrition, and adequate rest. It's not just about being free from illness; fitness is also about having the energy, strength, and stamina to perform daily activities and engage in physical activities effectively.
